Time

T1_LO10 · lo-time

Complete
Key idea:

Time conversions use fixed facts: $1 h=60 min$, $1 min=60 s$, $1 day=24 h$. Elapsed time is the difference between a start and finish time.

Steps

  1. Convert to the same unit if needed.

  1. Use 24-hour time if it helps avoid mistakes.

  1. For elapsed time, count up from the start to the end.

  1. For finish time, add the duration to the start time.

Key facts

  • $2 h=120 min$

  • $3 min=180 s$

  • $15{:}30$ is 3:30 pm

  • $90 min=1 h 30 min$

Common mistake

Treating 2.5 hours as 2 hours 50 minutes. Since 0.5 of an hour is half of 60 minutes, 2.5 hours is 2 hours 30 minutes.

Try these

  1. Convert $150 min$ to hours and minutes.

  2. Convert 7:15 pm to 24-hour time.

  3. Find the time 45 minutes after 2:30 pm.
