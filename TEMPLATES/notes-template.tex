\CommonMistake{Mixing unlike terms, for example writing \(2x + 3 = 5x\). The \(x\) and the constant are not like terms.}

\TryThese{%
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Simplify \(4x + 2x\)
  \item Expand \(2(x+5)\)
  \item Simplify \(7a - 3a\)
\end{enumerate}}
